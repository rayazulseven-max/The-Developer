Clinical-Grade Data Integrity

In medical billing, an AI hallucination isn't just an error—it's a denied claim. I engineered a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) system grounded in professional medical coding standards.

The Challenge

Generic LLMs often fail to distinguish between similar HCPCS Level II codes, leading to inaccurate medical supply mapping.

The Solution

A Weighted Semantic Search engine with 80% confidence thresholds and regex interceptors for zero-latency code lookups.

DOMAINE EXPERTISE: AAPC CERTIFIED

Leveraging my AAPC Professional Medical Billing background, I implemented strict data validation rules that prevent the engine from guessing when clinical data is ambiguous or unavailable.
